## 🧬 About Me

> *"Quality is not an act, it is a habit."* — I build systems where quality is engineered in, not bolted on.

I'm a **Senior Quality Engineer** based in Stockholm with **7+ years** of experience designing and owning test strategies across **web, mobile, desktop, and embedded platforms**. I specialise in building scalable automation frameworks, integrating quality into CI/CD pipelines, and driving shift-left practices across cross-functional engineering teams.

From validating complex micro-frontend web applications to forensic mobile software and real-time embedded systems — I bring both the technical depth and the strategic thinking to make quality a team-wide discipline.
